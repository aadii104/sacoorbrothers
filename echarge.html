HISTORY

Sacoor Brothers was founded in 1989 at number 127, Rua Pascoal de Melo, Lisbon. Sacoor began by selling articles for men and women, on its single selling point. In 1990, Sacoor Brothers opened its second store at Rua Pedro Nunes, also in Lisbon and its first store in a shopping center in 1997. Colombo was the chosen shopping center and led the way to a string of success that enabled the brand to achieve a sales volume never seen before. At one time, stock that was to last six months was sold out in just two weeks! The brand garnered more visibility due to its presence across the city, making it known among the general public. The success continued the following years ahead, with new stores opening across the country. In 2001, Sacoor Brothers opened its first Sacoor Woman store and finally started its international expansion in 2007. Sacoor Brothers is present in several countries. Today the brand is internationally recognized in very different markets. Sacoor Brothers carefully chooses the location for each new store. Shopping centers must be well located and have a strong flow of customers. Sacoor Brothers is emerging as a brand of excellence with a distinct concept of personalized service, quality and product innovation provided to its customers.

COMPANY

Sacoor Brothers is an international lifestyle brand that offers a variety of styles for you to transition seamlessly from work week to weekend. We offer collections for the sophisticated man, elegant woman and fashionable child that comprise apparel, quality footwear and accessories. With over 20 years of experience in the fashion industry, Sacoor Brothers has reached an important role in the fashion business in setting trends, fashion-forward products and services. It is through our continuous improvement that we achieved strong recognition in the markets and segments where it is positioned.

KAIZEN

At Sacoor Brothers, we work under the Kaizen philosophy. It is believed that you can always do better and that no day should pass without some improvement implemented, whether in the structure of the company or the individual. This methodology brings factual results, both qualitative and quantitative, in a short time and at a low cost. Thus, it strengthens the team spirit, promoting the motivation of all employees, who come together around common goals.

THE NEW SACOOR CLUB

The Sacoor Club allows you to win Points with every purchase you make in all our stores. The more Points you earn, the more rewards and benefits you can enjoy. There are two types of Points in the Sacoor Club program, which are valued in different ways. Reward Points Can be redeemed for additional discount on your purchases and are earned in all your purchases. Tier Points Are collected when you purchase and help you reach the next tier of membership to enter a wider world of benefits. Our loyalty program is free to join and will make your shopping experience with us even more pleasurable. Membership gives you the chance to earn and spend Points with Sacoor Brothers and all our other boutiques, and also enhances your shopping experience with services that make every visit to our stores more rewarding.

OR

EARNING POINTS

The number of Tier Points you earn is equal to the total value of your receipt, net of all deductions and discounts. They cannot be spent and are only used to build up your tier status from Blue to Silver, Gold, or Platinum.

Reward Points
Every time you shop with Sacoor Brothers you earn Reward Points. The Reward Points you earn when shopping from Sacoor Brothers are calculated using two inputs: ticket value and tier. So the more you spend, the more Reward Points you win. Once you reach the Silver, Gold and Platinum tiers you also begin collecting more Points.
Tier Points
You also earn Tier Points in addition to Reward Points every time you shop at Sacoor Brothers. They cannot be spent and are only used to build up your tier status from Blue, through Silver and Gold, to Platinum. Your Tier Points are calculated using your ticket value only, net of all deductions and discounts.

USING POINTS

Spend Reward Points on your style, from your favorite Sacoor Brothers collection items or by gifting your friends with our Sacoor Kids line. We do not apply any blackout dates to use your Reward Points, which gives you total flexibility on how to use them. Tier Points are credited to your account immediately after you have made a purchase. Reward Points are credited in your account on the following calendar day after your purchase. How to use your Reward Points? Your Reward Points are available on the following day after your purchase. Spending Reward Points is as easy as earning it. Each Reward Point you have is worth 1 monetary unit. Hence, you may choose to pay your purchase in one of two ways: REWARD POINTS OR REWARD POINTS + CASH

YOUR ACCOUNT

An easy-to-use, personalized My Account greets every Sacoor Club member after log in. It is straightforward to navigate and lets you see your total Points, recent transactions and lots of other useful information at a glance. To help you manage every aspect of your account, My Account acts as a hub of convenient links to relevant pages. Key alerts keep you up to date and it even shows you special offers relating to your tier.
